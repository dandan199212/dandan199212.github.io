What does it take to be successful in life?

 

How do different countries see the intrinsic and extrinsic nature of success in life?

Does success depend more on intrinsic factors, such as hard work and good education,
OR extrinsic factors, such as family, social network, gender, or even giving bribes?

 

Countries in different continents demonstrate skewed views on factors that contribute to success.

  • North American and South American countries find education, hard work, social network very important.
  • In addition to education, hard work, and social network, Asian countires attribute more significace to luck.
  • Collectively, African countries demonstrate a strong tendency to see luck as essential to success.
  • European countries have a more balanced view on the intrinsic and extrinsic nature of success.
  • The belief in the importance of being male to success is stronger in Africa, South America, and Asia.

 

Survey data from Pew Research Center: Spring 2017 Survey.
